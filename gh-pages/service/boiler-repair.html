Signs You Need Boiler Repair

The classic warning signs are easy to miss until it's too late. Radiators that take forever to warm up, banging or kettling sounds from the boiler, a pilot light that keeps going out, or a pressure gauge that won't stay between 1 and 1.5 bar are all early signs something's not right. Yellow flames instead of blue, or a faint smell of gas, mean you should stop using the boiler and call us straight away.

Waiting almost always costs more. A small leak left for a month can rust a heat exchanger that costs hundreds to replace. Cold, damp West of Scotland winters mean a broken boiler quickly turns into burst pipes. If your boiler is over 12 years old and breaking down regularly, repair vs replacement is a real conversation — and one we'll have honestly with you.
